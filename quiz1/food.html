Food of Tangerang

Introducing 4 local food of Tangerang:

Laksa Tangerang

Laksa is a food that has the combination of Malay and Chinese taste. This particular food is popular in some Asian Countries, especially in Malaysia which Laksa Penang is very popular. Indonesia has some variation of Laksa, one of which is Laksa Tangerang. It has noodle,bean sprouts, and a thick yellow soup. It has become one of Tangerang most favourite food.

Dodol Ny. Lauw

Dodol is an Indonesian sweet toffee-like sugar palm-based confection. It is made from coconut milk, jaggery, and rice flour, and is sticky, thick, and sweet. Dodol Ny. Lauw is a Tangerang specialty and has unique flavour.

Es Kode

This is actually not a food, but a restaurant's name. It mainly sells juice. What makes it special? The juice served all has a big portion, and it's made from fresh fruit only. They make the juice in front of you, and they don't need to add sugar to make it sweet. This is one of Tangerang most famous restaurant.

Kecap Benteng

Kecap is Indonesian sweet soy sauce. It's very common to be found in any Indonesian foods. Tangerang has its special kecap, which is called kecap benteng. It starts producing in 1920's, and has become Tangerang's people choice since than. You can found this kecap used in many street food vendor and restaurant in Tangerang.
