Where to go in Tangerang?

TOURIST ATTRACTIONS

Tangerang is mostly a metropolitan area, which means the road can be heavily traffic congested. Some modern housing development like Gading Serpong, BSD, and Alam Sutra has a better trafic and offers a lot of attraction such as shopping malls, restaurants, and golf courses. If you're into cultrue and history, Pasar Lama area is a must go. It contains a lot of Tangerang oldest shops and houses. Here i will list some of Tangerang most popular tourist attractions.

IKEA

IKEA is an international company that designs and sells ready-to-assemble furniture, kitchen appliances and home accessories. IKEA Alam Sutra is the first and only (at the time of writing) IKEA shop in Indonesia. This has made it a popular spot to visit by people from all of Indonesia.

AEON BSD

AEON is a shopping mall group from Japan. It opens its first Indonesia mall in BSD. This shopping mall has a different concept from other Indonesia shopping malls. It offers a lot of Japanese foods and goods,as well as spacious parking spots which makes it one of the most visited mall in Indonesia.

Benteng Heritage Museum

Benteng Heritage Museum is a result of restoration of an old building with Chinese architecture. It is built in the 17th century, which makes it one of Tangerang oldest building. It is located in Pasar Lama area, which is where Tangerang city is formed. You can see some ancient artefacts and history of Chinese Indonesian in Tangerang.
